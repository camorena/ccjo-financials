Proposed Resolution

Three options to settle the outstanding balance and restore the business relationship

Option A: Full Settlement

$38,652.42

Carrieles SAS remits the full outstanding balance to CCJO Partners Trading LLC via wire transfer to settle all amounts for steel previously purchased and delivered. This includes $38,281.42 in steel costs plus $371.00 in wire transfer fees borne by CCJO.

Option B: Payment Plan

Structured Payments

Carrieles SAS agrees to a structured payment plan to settle the $38,652.42 balance over an agreed period (e.g., 3–6 monthly installments), with payments via wire transfer to CCJO's Wells Fargo account.

Option C: Resume & Settle

Settle + Continue

If both parties wish to continue the trading relationship: (1) Carrieles settles the outstanding $38,652.42 balance, (2) future transactions are structured with the agreed $5,000/container margin, and (3) payment terms are reduced from 5–7 months to 30–60 days.
